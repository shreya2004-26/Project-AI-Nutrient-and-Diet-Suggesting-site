About NutriDietFlow

What is NutriDietFlow?

NutriDietFlow is an advanced nutritional recommendation system powered by machine learning. Our platform uses a Random Forest model to provide personalized dietary suggestions based on your unique characteristics and preferences.

How Does It Work?

Our system analyzes multiple factors including:

  • Personal demographics (age, gender)
  • Physical characteristics (height, weight)
  • Lifestyle factors (activity level, weekly exercise)
  • Dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Medical conditions and food allergies
  • Health and fitness goals

Model Information

The core of NutriDietFlow is a Random Forest Regressor model trained on comprehensive nutritional data. Key features include:

  • Accuracy: 97.5% (RÂ² Score)
  • Robust prediction capability with minimal error rate
  • Consideration of multiple dietary patterns and health conditions
  • Personalized macro and micronutrient recommendations

What You Get

  • Personalized daily calorie recommendations
  • Detailed macro and micronutrient breakdown
  • Customized meal suggestions based on your dietary preferences
  • Considerations for medical conditions and allergies
  • Goal-oriented nutritional guidance

Important Note

While our recommendations are based on scientific data and advanced machine learning algorithms, they should not replace professional medical advice. Always consult with healthcare providers before making significant changes to your diet.
